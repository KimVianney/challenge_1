Introduction

There are very many careers in the ICT sector. They range from a number of fields like web designing to to software and hardware management. These careers are meant for the quick and steady development in the ICT sector world wide. We are going to dicuss the different careers in the sector on this page.

Web Designer

The role of a web designer is to plan, design and develop websites taking care of the entire development project including hosting of the website.The web designer also handles implementation of the website.

System Analyst

A system analyst is in charge of developing designs for a required system. A system analyst works hand in hand with a software engineer to come up with and implement a properly working system. A system analyst, however, is not required to have any skill in programming.

Graphics Designer

This person is responsible for designing and implementing visual effects on a computer. This career requires one to have very good knowledge of simulation and visual software and maybe some bit of creativity.

Network Administrator

A network administrator is in charge of setting up and managing network infrastructure. A network administrator is also in charge of troubleshooting network systems.

Forensic Specialist

A forensic specialist is in charge of maintaining and investigating crimes related to computers. Forensic specialists range from network to digital and mobile forensic specialists.

Computer Engineer

A computer engineer is incharge of planning, designing and developing computer hardware components. A computer engineer may or may not have knowledge in programming. However, they should have knowledge on electric circuits and components.

Computer Technician

A computer Technician repairs and fixes computer hardware components. However, a computer technician can also troubleshoot and fix bugs in software solutions.

Computer Teacher

A computer teacher is one who imparts knowledge of the use of computers and their components onto students. A computer teacher is required only to have knowledge about computers and additional knowledge about programming.
